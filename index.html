    Bengaluru · Part Time

    Marketing Strategist

    We are looking for a Marketing Strategist to work in collaboration with the founders and the design team. This is a key position fit for a person who loves crunching down numbers, perform market analysis, derive insights, plan out marketing and branding strategies, execute branding and advertising campaigns and evaluate them and repeat. A sharp mind with a dash of creativity and curiosity, handful of good communication skills and determination to achieving results - could be a perfect recipe of an ideal candidate.

    Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Develop accurate project priorities, schedules and timelines with a data-driven approach.
  • Work collaboratively with the founders, product team and design team.
  • Actively execute brand building campaign and brand development strategies.
  • Execute brand awareness through online and offline media.
  • Strategise pre-launch buzz creation, launch event planning and post-launch customer retention through online and offline marketing media.
  • Actively manage PR activities.
  • Constantly evaluate and measure the effectiveness of consumer promotion and retention strategies.

    • Skills and expertise:

  • 2+ experience in Brand Planning, Brand Management and Marketing.
  • A proven track record of creating and executing successful marketing plans which are creative, testable, analytical and engaging for new as well as existing product launches.
  • Demonstrate analytical skills in quantitative and qualitative research methodology and outputs, media channel strategy and plans.
  • Demonstrate identifying new brand development opportunities and possible environmental or competitive threats as well as building ideas that encompass every brand touch-point with customers.
  • Master’s degree in Business Administration, Economics, Marketing, Advertising or related fields is preferred.
